About TD LOANS

TD LOANS is a customer-first financial institution that understands your unique needs. With our headquarters in KwaZulu-Natal and branches across the province, we're committed to offering reliable loan services, funeral policies, and debt collection solutions.

Since our founding, TD LOANS has made it a priority to operate with integrity, speed, and a personal touch. We process applications fast, offer flexible terms, and ensure that every client is treated with dignity and care. Our team is passionate about providing financial freedom and support when you need it most.

Whether you're applying for a personal loan or exploring our additional services, we're here to guide you with clarity and compassion.

Our Mission

To deliver reliable, ethical, and customer-focused financial services that address the diverse needs of our community.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted financial partner for individuals and businesses across South Africa.
